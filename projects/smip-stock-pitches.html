SMIP Stock Buy/Sell Pitches

Team-based equity research project focused on developing structured buy and sell recommendations through technical and fundamental analysis.

Overview

This work was completed through the Student Managed Investment Portfolio, where research had to be clear enough to support real investment discussion rather than just classroom analysis.

  • Researched companies with teams of one to five to prepare structured investment recommendations.
  • Used technical signals, valuation context, and business fundamentals to support buy or sell positions.
  • Converted research into pitch materials built for challenge, discussion, and decision making.

What This Showcases

  • Financial analysis under team deadlines.
  • Ability to summarize a company and defend a position clearly.
  • Comfort working at the intersection of market research and presentation.
